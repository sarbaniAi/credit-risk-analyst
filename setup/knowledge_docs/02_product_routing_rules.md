# Product Routing Rules — Indian Retail Banking

## Product Eligibility Matrix

| Product | Eligible Tiers | Max Risk Score | Additional Criteria |
|---------|---------------|---------------|-------------------|
| Personal Loan | Tier 1, 2 | 40 | Income > INR 4,00,000, employment > 12 months |
| Home Loan | Tier 1, 2 | 35 | Employment > 24 months, asset coverage > 3× EMI |
| Vehicle Loan | Tier 1, 2, 3 | 45 | Income > INR 3,00,000, valid driving license |
| Credit Card (Unsecured) | Tier 1, 2, 3 | 50 | Income > INR 2,50,000, no active NPA |
| Secured Credit Card | Tier 3, 4 | 65 | FD/collateral of 120% of credit limit |
| Gold Loan | Any Tier | 80 | Physical gold verification at branch |
| Microfinance / JLG | Tier 3, 4 | 70 | Joint Liability Group formation, PSL eligible |
| Business Loan (MSME) | Tier 1, 2 | 40 | GST registration, 2-year ITR, Udyam certificate |

## Bundle Selection Rules

- **Premium Bundle** (Tier 1, Risk Score < 20): Pre-approved personal loan + Platinum credit card + Wealth management referral + Locker facility.
- **Growth Bundle** (Tier 2, Risk Score < 35): Personal loan + Credit card + Term insurance cross-sell.
- **Starter Bundle** (Tier 3, Risk Score < 50): Secured credit card + Recurring deposit + Micro-insurance.

## Loan Amount Limits

- Personal Loan: Min INR 50,000, Max INR 25,00,000 (up to 10× monthly income)
- Home Loan: Max 80% of property value (90% for properties up to INR 30,00,000 per RBI norms)
- Vehicle Loan: Max 85% of on-road price for new vehicles, 70% for used vehicles
- Gold Loan: Max 75% of gold value (as per RBI LTV norms)
- MSME Loan: Up to INR 2,00,00,000 under CGTMSE guarantee scheme
