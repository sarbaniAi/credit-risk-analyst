# RBI Compliance & Regulatory Checklist for Credit Decisions

## 1. IRAC Norms — Asset Classification (RBI Master Directions)

| Category | Definition | Overdue Period | Provisioning |
|----------|-----------|---------------|-------------|
| Standard | No default in repayment | Current | 0.40% |
| SMA-0 | Principal/interest overdue 1–30 days | 1–30 days | Report to CRILC |
| SMA-1 | Principal/interest overdue 31–60 days | 31–60 days | Report + Watch |
| SMA-2 | Principal/interest overdue 61–90 days | 61–90 days | Report + Review |
| Sub-Standard | NPA for ≤ 12 months | > 90 days overdue | 15% |
| Doubtful (up to 1 year) | NPA for > 12 months | > 12 months NPA | 25% |
| Doubtful (1–3 years) | NPA for 1–3 years | 1–3 years NPA | 40% |
| Doubtful (> 3 years) | NPA for > 3 years | > 3 years NPA | 100% |
| Loss | Identified as loss by bank/auditor | Irrecoverable | 100% |

## 2. Fair Practices Code for Lenders

- All rejection reasons must be communicated in writing within 15 working days.
- Interest rate methodology must be transparent and non-discriminatory.
- No lending discrimination based on caste, religion, gender, or marital status.
- Prepayment/foreclosure charges: Nil for floating rate loans (RBI circular DNBR.CC.PD.No.067/03.10.01/2014-15).
- Loan agreement must contain the Most Important Terms and Conditions (MITC) in the language understood by the borrower.
- Recovery agents must carry proper authorization and follow dignity norms.

## 3. Priority Sector Lending (PSL) Targets

- Overall PSL: 40% of Adjusted Net Bank Credit (ANBC) for domestic banks.
- Agriculture: 18% of ANBC (Small & Marginal Farmers: 10% of ANBC).
- Micro Enterprises: 7.5% of ANBC.
- Advances to Weaker Sections: 12% of ANBC.
- Housing: Loans up to INR 35,00,000 in metros / INR 25,00,000 in other areas qualify as PSL.

## 4. Escalation Procedures

| Trigger | Escalate To | TAT |
|---------|------------|-----|
| Risk Score > 70 + Income > INR 10L (high value decline) | Branch Manager | Same day |
| Customer disputes risk classification | L2 Credit Analyst + Compliance | 2 business days |
| Suspected fraud indicators | Fraud Investigation Unit | Immediate |
| Loan amount > Branch Manager authority (> INR 25L) | Regional Credit Committee | 5 business days |
| NPA migration (SMA-2 to NPA) | Asset Recovery Management | Within 24 hours |
| Regulatory inquiry on a credit decision | Chief Risk Officer + Legal | Immediate |

## 5. Data Field Reference

- **Education codes**: 0=Below 10th, 1=10th Pass, 2=12th Pass, 3=Graduate, 4=Post-Graduate, 5=Professional (CA/CS/CFA/MBBS/LLB)
- **Marital status codes**: 0=Single, 1=Married, 2=Divorced, 3=Widowed
- **Prediction field**: 1=High Credit Risk (likely to default), 0=Low Credit Risk (likely to repay)
- **Income amounts**: All in INR (Indian Rupees)
- **CIBIL score range**: 300–900 (750+ is Good, 650–750 is Fair, below 650 is Poor)
